Introduction

The as-purchased design of the pants were restrictively shallow. Sitting with a phone in the front pocket lead to pressure on the hip and stress against the pocket lining. Using available materials, I was able to increase the pocket capacity three fold.

Process

The first (right) pocket was modified in a more freeform way.
The second (left) pocket was modified in a more methodical way, using strategies learned from modifying the first pocket. Initial planning allowed for the second pocket to begin in a neater fashion, leading to compounding improvements and superior final result.
